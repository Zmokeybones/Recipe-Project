Honey Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 (6oz each) salmon filets
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika (or regular paprika)
  • 1/4 tsp blackening seasonin (optional)

Sauce

  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 3 Tbsp water
  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp siracha sauce
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
